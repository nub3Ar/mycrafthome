Quote Analysis Service


Are you looking to renovate and want to ensure that you are receiving the best possible quote? Let us help you analyze it for you!

We truly believe that homeowners that are empowered with the necessary knowledge of navigating the quote process will choose a more fitting contractor and have a more successful home renovation process, and we want to help get you there!

Therefore, we have developed a product that will comb through your quote and potential contract to analyze it. We also have a panel of trusted contractors and designers that are ready to look over your quote. Each quote analysis comes with:

  • Contractor Check
  • Milestone Check
  • Payment Schedule Timeline Estimation
